Josue Moran

 About | Contact

Josue Moran is a 19-year-old Mexican- American artist who enjoys the diversity that engulfs him in New York City. He submerges himself in different parts of New York City to broaden his art and his way of thinking. While exploring, he had the pleasure of working closely with the CEO of Lightbox-NY and most recently Red Hooks Labs. Josue spends most of his time at Red Hooks Labs, where he interns as a studio assistant and attends photography classes to improve his artwork. From choosing an emulsion, capturing a photo, and the development process, Josue discovered an appreciation for the film medium because of the slow process to get the perfect image. All this excites Josue, as it is a constant process that must be trusted in order to view the final work.
